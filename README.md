Family Fantasy Football Clubhouse 🏈

A kid-friendly fantasy football museum for our family league.

This project preserves the history of our family fantasy football league while turning it into something playful, colorful, and fun for the kids to explore over time.

It is part museum, part scrapbook, part trophy room, part Saturday morning cartoon.

What Is This?

The Family Fantasy Football Clubhouse is a long-term home for our family fantasy football league.

It tracks:

* Champions
* Seasons
* Team names
* Rivalries
* Draft memories
* Funny awards
* Achievements
* Family league history

The goal is not just to store standings.

The goal is to remember the stories.

Who Plays?

Current league members:

* Shawn
* Jennifer
* Daphne
* Elliot

Future league member:

* Wyatt, when he is old enough to understand the game

League Format

This is a simplified family fantasy football league.

* Redraft format
* Snake draft
* Simplified rosters
* Simplified scoring
* Built for kids and family fun

Project Vibe

The app should feel like:

* Saturday morning cartoons
* Backyard Football
* ESPN for Kids
* Pixar-style family fun
* A colorful football clubhouse
* A family scrapbook with trophies

This is not meant to feel like a serious fantasy analytics tool.

It should feel fun, readable, visual, and personal.

Core Goals

Preserve History

Every season should become part of the family record.

Who won?
Who almost won?
Who had the funniest team?
Who started a rivalry?
Who caused chaos?

Celebrate the Kids

The site should make the league feel exciting for Daphne, Elliot, and eventually Wyatt.

Even if someone does not win the championship, they should still have fun awards, badges, moments, and memories.

Track Rivalries

Over time, the app should help us build family fantasy football rivalries.

Examples:

* Daphne vs Elliot
* Shawn vs Daphne
* Shawn vs Jennifer
* Jennifer vs Elliot
* Eventually Wyatt vs everyone

Make It Fun to Revisit

Years from now, the kids should be able to open the site and laugh at:

* Old team names
* Silly awards
* Championship runs
* Draft mistakes
* Family jokes
* Rivalry records
